About Us

Mangan & associates is an emerging challenger brand to traditional recruitment services in financial services. We specialise in fuelling momentum for faster-growth investment Managers, Fund Managers, Superannuation Funds, insurers, Banks, and FinTechs with exceptional talent.

Services

Drawing on a rich, high-performance network, we design and deliver high-quality customised talent solutions to help clients solve business problems and create positive outcomes. Years of experience mean we understand complex commercial landscapes, know how to navigate those environments, and offer sound independent advice to create long-lasting productive talent solutions.
